- D-06: Active CSRF enforcement does not rely on `csurf`.
- D-07: CSRF exemptions are limited to safe GET paths, token creation, and OAuth GET paths.
- D-08: Frontend CSRF headers are attached in shared Axios code.

- D-15: Reset records stay single-active, 5-minute, non-enumerating, and single-use.
- D-16: OAuth redirects use a strict frontend origin allowlist.
- D-17: User-provided OAuth redirect targets are ignored.
- D-23: Phase keeps the approved three-plan split.
