## Accomplishments

- Required `clientMessageId` in `newMessage` so browser-originated creates cannot bypass the idempotency contract.

## Decisions Made

- Diagnostics are guarded by `CHATIFY_DELIVERY_DIAGNOSTICS=1` so normal test and production output stays quiet unless delivery tracing is deliberately enabled.
- The backend keeps `chat:join` as an active selected-chat fallback, but connection-time room auto-join remains receive-only and does not mark historical messages delivered.
