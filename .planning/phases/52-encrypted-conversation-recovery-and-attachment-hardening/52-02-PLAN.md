# Phase 52 Plan 02: Conversation Recovery UI

## Objective

Expose recovery-key copy/import inside the encrypted conversation detail surface.

## Tasks

1. Add an encrypted recovery panel to `ConversationDetailContent`.

5. Add component tests for encrypted and standard conversations.
